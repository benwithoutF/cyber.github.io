About Us

The CoE in Cyber Security was formed in 2018 by Sahyadri College of Engineering and Managment , as part of the Technology Innovation Strategy, As we know the cybercrime is one of the ill full effects in the society causing new threat for common man. Many resources have given alarming inputs that the cybercrime rates have been increased 5 times in last four years. These may escalate exponentially further, if these are not prevented by technological interventions. For making this possible we felt there is a need of well-equipped cyber security and forensics lab in this coastal area of Karnataka. Centre of Excellence in Cyber-security is formed to address the skill gap, awareness and technological innovation by a well-equipped cyber security and forensics lab in this coastal area of Karnataka.

VISION & MISSION

Our Vision is to deliver cyber security solutions and services to Secure Digital Process and enterprise.
Mission: -Identify and address the critical challenges in cybersecurity. –Develop quality forensics and cyber Solution to provide training and service to stockholders. -Transform research results in sustainable technologies and product development.

OBJECTIVES

EDUCATIONAL SUPPORT To create a collaborative working environment between university, government agencies, and commercial establishment, to share information on potential threats, counter-measures for such actions through ongoing research projects. GOVT. SUPPORT To support the law enforcement agencies in technical matters and also to help them in the development of cyber laws, whenever required in their investigations. Also, support them in the capacity building. RESEARCH To promote research in the upcoming areas of Digital Forensics and Cybersecurity to provide solutions to cybercrime and cybersecurity-related issues and To publish scientific books, magazines, and compilations. TRAINING To conduct training and skill development that can aid to understand the nature of cybercrime .Conducting seminars, national and international conferences to create awareness among the stakeholders.
